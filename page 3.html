Rohit Sharma - The Hitman

About Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian international cricketer known for his explosive batting style. He holds the record for the highest individual score in One Day Internationals (ODIs), scoring 264 against Sri Lanka in 2014. Rohit Sharma is often referred to as "The Hitman" for his ability to score big runs and deliver in crucial moments.

Career Highlights

Rohit Sharma has been a key player for the Indian cricket team, contributing significantly to their success in limited-overs cricket. His double centuries in ODIs and multiple centuries in T20 internationals showcase his remarkable talent and consistency.
